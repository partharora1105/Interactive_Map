One IFC and Two IFC

Author's Note

In the text, a short history of our shores, Xi writes:
'Two companion towers, IFC One and Two rose out of the earth–cold, phallic, curving upwards into an uncircumcised foreskin–in all their silvery pallor.’ (108)
When asked for further insight into that description, she commented:
When I wrote that line, I was thinking about how I'd long disliked the buildings and found them a blight on our skyline (of course many said the same of the World Trade Center Towers in NYC when they first went up but when I came to the city to live in the 1980's, those two building oriented me and I came to love them. I still sorely miss them because when 9-11 happened, I was living in Manhattan).
